Plazza

Fixed Assets & Facility Management Software. Plazza helps organizations find space in their facilities

Schedule Demo

Features

Plazza is offered as a hosted solution in the cloud or installed on premise.

Fixed Asset Management

  • Manage Fixed Assets and their Location
  • Track assets preventive maintenance and repair
  • Service Calendar (“intervention” schedule)
  • Dashboard with upcoming interventions
  • Highly Flexible Assets page through custom fields
  • Track any equipment-specific information
  • Generate report on asset location, categories, repairs
  • Barcode support

Building & Floor Planning

  • Define your own Space/Architecture:
    • Building → Floor → Room → People
  • Keep Track of Keys and People
  • View Floor Plan and occupation
  • Capacity Planner (coming in 2017)
    • Find desk available within your organization
    • Ease the onboarding process
  • Get Rooms by Occupancy

Equipment Booking

  • Ability to share equipment with others departments/services/labs
  • Manage Booking Requests
  • Manage Booking Calendar
    • Self-serve Booking
    • Suit any type of equipment (scientific, common equipment, appliance...)
  • Generate Usage Report
    • Track who borrows what

Notifications

  • Ability to send targeted notification to certain people only
  • Manage any type of notification
    • Construction
    • Shutdown
    • Key Holders
    • ...
  • Define and re-use notification template

And More

  • Users Management
    • LDAP Integration (Active Directory - on-premise only)
  • Web-based Solution
  • Web API
  • Reports
  • Integrate with your systems & workflow
  • Bilingual Application (English & French)
  • Roadmap Available
  • Custom Development Service offered to tailor your specific needs
  • Support and Online Help
