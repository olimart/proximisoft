Laborder

Inventory and Warehouse Management Software.
Laborder targets the Scientific and Research Community and is adopted by Universities

Schedule Demo

Features

Laborder is offered as a hosted solution in the cloud or installed on premise.

Product Inventory

  • Manage any type of product/goods
    • Perishable
    • Radioactive/Hazardous
  • Set and Monitor Quantity On Hand (QOH) across multiple warehouses
  • Manage product visibility and storages
  • Product Catalog
    • Save product to favorites for quick access
    • Compare products
  • Barcode support (inventory through barcode scanner)

Requisition

  • Labs submit requisition:
    • Free-form requisition
    • From product in inventory
  • Approval process between buyer (lab technician for instance) and account delegate (Principle Investigator for instance)
  • Workflow process between buyer and procurement services to validate requisition
  • Assign requisition to procurement user for ordering
  • Manage requisition life cycle and order status
  • Ability to handle multiple carts shared within lab

Receiving

  • Track package reception and replenishment
  • Track package pickup or in-floor delivery

System Administration

  • Users Management
    • LDAP Integration (Active Directory - on-premise only)
    • Assign role and permissions to users
  • Manage suppliers
    • Manage promotions
  • Manage storages and warehouses
  • Manage carriers
  • Manage services and users

Lab/Service/Department

  • Personalized dashboard for all users
  • Access frequent resources at a fingertip
  • Orders history and overview
    • Ability to re-order
    • Powerful search

And More

  • Web-based Solution
  • Web API
  • Reports
  • Integrate with your systems & workflow
  • Bilingual Application (English & French)
  • Roadmap Available
  • Custom Development Service offered to tailor your specific needs
