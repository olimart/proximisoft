About Us

Proximisoft specialize in the conception and the development of productivity tools.

We empower the framework Ruby on Rails to build innovative solutions for mobile & desktop that are accessible anytime anywhere.

We've built numerous inventory and warehouse management solutions for medium to large companies in a broad range of industries such as education, e-commerce, dairy companies, newspapers and cut flowers.

We connect systems and organizations altogether, re-engineer processes, automate tasks to save your team time, money and energy.

Our missions include connecting back-end systems and ERPs to accounting systems, synchronize stocks across multiple warehouses, onboard users, implement point-of-sale solutions, and globally speaking, help companies become more effective.

Services

We cover the full spectrum of software development, starting with product vision to the build and delivery phase.

We are Agile and believe in short iterations. This means, we share with clients early and often to ensure that we remain on the same page.

Our process is simple and easy to follow:

  1. Business Analysts take the time to understand your business, process, pain points and the expected result
  2. We translate all your needs into functional specifications, come up with a plan and then move on to the build phase
  3. From there, expect regular progress update with live screens you can interact with, validate internally and share with others. We will refine the system based on your feedback and our expertise until the final product
